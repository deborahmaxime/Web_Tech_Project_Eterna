About the Project

Eterna

Eterna is a web application for preserving personal stories and memories. It allows users to create an interactive timeline where each entry is a "capsule" containing images, videos, dates, locations, and written narratives.

In today's digital age, people store photos and videos across multiple platforms, but these memories become disorganized over time. The stories behind the photos are often forgotten. There's no personal, meaningful way to preserve a full narrative for family or posterity.
