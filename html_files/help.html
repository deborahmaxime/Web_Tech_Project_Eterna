‚ùì More frequently Asked Questions

How do I recover a deleted memory?
Don't worry! Deleted memories go to your "Archive" for 30 days before being permanently deleted. You can restore them anytime within that period from your Dashboard settings.
Can I share memories with non-ETERNA users?
Absolutely! When you share a memory, we generate a special link that anyone can view for 7 days. They don't need an account to see your shared memories.
Is there a limit to how many memories I can store?
Free accounts get 500 memories, while Premium users enjoy unlimited storage! Plus, we automatically optimize your photos to save space without losing quality.
How do future messages work?
Future messages are like time capsules! You write them now, set a delivery date, and we'll send them to you (or someone else) on that special day in the future. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or notes to your future self!
